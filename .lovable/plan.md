

# Desktop UX Audit & Redesign + Persona-Driven Demo Data + History Visualization

## Current Problems

1. **Dashboard doesn't drive action.** The landing page shows community posts and announcements with equal weight as the Snapshot CTA. For most users, the #1 reason to log in is to take their Snapshot — the dashboard should funnel them there.

2. **Snapshot page shows history BEFORE scoring.** Users see their previous scores in the radar chart and score summary bar before they rate, biasing their new scores. The user explicitly wants a "fresh eyes" experience — rate first, then reveal history.

3. **History visualization is a spaghetti line chart.** 11 lines on one chart is unreadable. Need a per-category visualization that lets you quickly see improvement and remember what you did to improve.

4. **Mock data doesn't match the target persona.** Current base scores are generic. Need a specific "successful business owner, great dad, struggles with marriage due to travel
